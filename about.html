About Us

Our Story

From frustration to foundation

Meridian Digital was founded in 2018 in a cramped Brooklyn coffee shop by two agency veterans who were done with the status quo. After years of watching brands pour budget into campaigns that looked good in pitch decks but delivered zero ROI, Sarah Jenkins and Mark Thompson decided there had to be a better way. They built Meridian on a simple premise: marketing should work, or it's not worth doing.

What started as a two-person consultancy quickly grew as clients realized the difference between surface-level metrics and real business impact. Today, we're a team of 30+ strategists, creatives, and analysts united by the same belief that got us started: your marketing budget is an investment, not an expense.
